# Barber Queue — Logo Redesign Contract R1

Status: **human-approved concept production; candidates only; no runtime promotion**

## Brand strategy

- Product stage: one-shop idea-validation web app that may later become a sellable product.
- Audience: Thai barber-shop owner first, customer second.
- Personality: friendly, practical, modern, trustworthy, calm, and easy to operate.
- Core promise: queue work moves in a clear order with less interruption and confusion.
- Concept thesis: **queue flow + precise cut**.
- Preferred lead: `Q Path`.

## Lineage boundary

The existing 3D pastel icon and rejected SVG pack are context/history only. Do not use their pixels, silhouette, construction, or motifs as visual input.

Forbidden motifs:

- barber pole;
- queue-ticket illustration;
- moustache;
- scissors badge;
- vintage barber crest;
- literal shopfront/chair mascot;
- generic sparkle/SaaS gradient.

## Candidate family

Create exactly three related but independently usable vector candidates:

1. `Q Path` — an abstract Q built from a clear queue path and one precise interruption/cut.
2. `Queue Fold` — ordered queue movement folding into a compact recognizable mark.
3. `BQ Motion` — a reduced B/Q monogram expressing handoff or forward movement.

Do not create three unrelated logo genres.

## Visual system

- Flat source-owned SVG geometry; no generated raster pretending to be a vector logo.
- Strong silhouette and balanced negative space.
- Maximum two brand colors plus neutral background.
- Candidate palette: cocoa `#3A2D25`, apricot `#E7A77C`, sage `#8EAA87`, warm cream `#FFF8EE`.
- Mark must also work as single-color white and single-color cocoa.
- No wordmark baked into the canonical mark SVG.
- Avoid tiny gaps, hairline detail, fake depth, texture, gradients, shadows, and rounded-square app-icon backing inside the mark itself.

## Required output

All candidate work stays under `docs/assets/logo-redesign-r1/`:

- `q-path.svg`
- `queue-fold.svg`
- `bq-motion.svg`
- `comparison.html`
- `comparison.png`
- `manifest.md`

The comparison board must show each candidate:

- large on warm cream;
- reversed white on dark cocoa;
- at `16px`, `40px`, and `192px`;
- in a simple app-header lockup reading `Barber Queue`;
- without fake product claims or extra brand copy.

## QA and promotion

- Browser-render the SVGs; do not judge them only from source markup.
- Check optical balance, closed/open negative spaces, crisp curves, and recognition at 16px.
- Check light/dark and monochrome behavior.
- Label all three `candidate/reference`.
- Stop for Kiattisak to select a winner.
- Do not replace `app/src/app/icon.png`, favicon, Apple/PWA icons, manifests, headers, or production assets in this pass.
