## Shared brand brief

- Product: a practical queue assistant for one neighborhood barber shop.
- Brand mood: **Friendly Practical Modern**.
- Meaning to explore: orderly turns, forward movement, a clean handoff, less waiting confusion, and precise craft.
- Palette: cocoa `#3A2D25`, apricot `#E7A77C`, sage `#8EAA87`, warm cream `#FFF8EE`; use at most three colors in a mark.
- Desired character: approachable, useful, memorable, compact, calm, and locally human—not luxury, macho, retro, or generic SaaS.

## Hard exclusions

Generated output must be new raster source exploration—not procedural SVG/Canvas/Pillow/ImageMagick art presented as imagegen.

## Three independent directions

Each direction gets exactly one fresh Image Gen call and zero retry in R1. Each lane must stay visually independent and write only to its own folder.

### A — Flow Notch

Create one asymmetric geometric symbol built from a continuous route interrupted by one confident crafted notch. It should express orderly motion plus a precise handoff without forming a letter, arrow, or magnifier.

Output folder: `docs/assets/logo-imagegen-r1/direction-a-flow-notch/`

### B — Turn System

Create one compact symbol where several equal queue units resolve into one clear next position. Use rhythm, spacing, and one decisive change of state; avoid literal people, dots-in-a-line, numbered tokens, or arrows.

Output folder: `docs/assets/logo-imagegen-r1/direction-b-turn-system/`

### C — Neighbor Handoff

Create one warm, ownable symbol from two complementary abstract pieces passing responsibility/space between them. It should suggest human turn-taking and local service without hands, people icons, hearts, speech bubbles, or letters.

Output folder: `docs/assets/logo-imagegen-r1/direction-c-neighbor-handoff/`

## Image composition contract

Each provider-generated raw image should be a clean identity study on exact warm cream:

- one single mark shown large as the primary focus;
- the same mark repeated only as small one-color cocoa and reversed-white proofs in two simple flat swatches;
- generous clear space and no clipping;
- flat vector-like edges, substantial shapes, open negative space, and a silhouette that could plausibly survive a later 16px redraw;
- no labels or explanatory text.

The large primary mark is the concept authority. Small repeats are consistency/reference evidence only; Image Gen may redraw details between repeats and those differences must be reported honestly.

## Required lane evidence

Each lane must preserve:

- untouched provider-generated raw PNG;
- exact final prompt text;
- provider/model/tool statement proving image generation was used;
- raw dimensions, color mode, SHA-256, call count, and retry count;
- `receipt.md` marking the asset `raw-imagegen/concept-reference` and listing visible strengths, drift, exclusions, and whether the mark is coherent enough for human comparison.

If no raw generated raster or provider receipt exists, the lane is blocked and cannot be counted as Image Gen.
