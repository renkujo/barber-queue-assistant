## Shared design brief

- One unmistakable capital **Q** only.
- Friendly Practical Modern: approachable, useful, compact, calm, adult.
- Ownable silhouette before conceptual explanation.
- Substantial flat geometry, controlled negative space, and a tail/cut that is structurally integrated.
- The mark must not be a simple circle plus diagonal handle, detached square, magnifying glass, speech bubble, power icon, map pin, or generic app symbol.
- The Q may be asymmetric, non-circular, or use a custom counter; it must remain recognizably Q.
- Exact flat warm-cream `#FFF8EE` canvas.
- Cocoa `#3A2D25` primary; optional single apricot `#E7A77C` or sage `#8EAA87` integrated state; at most three filled colors.
- One centered primary mark occupying about 55–65% of a square canvas with generous clear space.
- Flat vector-logo rendering intent; plausible later 16px redraw.

## Three independent Q treatments

### A — Carved Counter Q

Create a substantial non-circular Q from one primary mass. Make the counter and exit feel deliberately carved from the same form. The tail should emerge from internal negative space, not attach externally like a magnifier handle. Aim for calm, architectural friendliness rather than tech sharpness.

Folder: `docs/assets/logo-q-imagegen-r3/direction-a-carved-counter/`

### B — Soft Turn Q

Create a Q from one broad continuous rounded stroke whose weight or direction changes once to express “your turn.” The tail remains fused into the stroke and should feel like a natural release, not an arrow or punctuation. Aim for the friendliest, most human silhouette of the round without becoming childish or bubbly.

Folder: `docs/assets/logo-q-imagegen-r3/direction-b-soft-turn/`

### C — Joined Tail Q

Create a geometric Q where the tail crosses or joins the bowl through a distinctive internal junction. Use one clear cut/slot to create ownership and queue-order meaning. Avoid perfect-circle geometry, corporate monogram styling, and sharp fintech aggression.

Folder: `docs/assets/logo-q-imagegen-r3/direction-c-joined-tail/`
